FKF Meru County League Rules & Regulations

General Rules
  1. All matches shall be played in accordance with FIFA Laws of the Game.
  2. Each team must register a minimum of 15 players and maximum of 30 players.
  3. All players must be registered with the league before participating in any match.
  4. Team registration fee is KSh 16000 payable via M-Pesa.
  5. Matches shall be played as per the published fixture list.
Disciplinary Rules
Card Offenses:
  • Yellow Card: Warning to player
  • Two Yellow Cards in one match: Automatic red card and 2-match suspension
  • Direct Red Card: 3-match suspension
  • 6 Accumulated Yellow Cards: 1-match suspension
Other Offenses:
  • Violent conduct: Minimum 5-match suspension
  • Assault on match official: Minimum 2-year suspension
  • Match fixing: Lifetime ban from all FKF activities
Match Rules
  • Matches shall be 90 minutes duration (45 minutes each half)
  • Each team must have at least 7 players to start a match
  • Maximum of 5 substitutions per match
  • All substitutions must be made through the fourth official
  • Teams must report to the match venue at least 2 hours before kickoff
