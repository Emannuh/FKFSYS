About FKF Meru County League

Our Mission

The FKF Meru County League aims to promote football development at the grassroots level in Meru County. We provide a structured league system that nurtures talent, promotes fair play, and creates opportunities for players to showcase their skills.

League Structure

The league is divided into three zones:

  • Zone A: Northern Region
  • Zone B: Central Region
  • Zone C: Southern Region

Each team plays home and away matches within their zone, with the top teams qualifying for inter-zonal playoffs at the end of the season.

Registration Process

  1. Complete team registration form online
  2. Add player details (minimum 15, maximum 30 players)
  3. Pay registration fee of KSh 1,000 via M-Pesa
  4. Wait for admin approval and zone assignment
  5. Receive fixtures and start playing
