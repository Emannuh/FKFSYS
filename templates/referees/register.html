Referee Registration Terms & Conditions
Agreement to Terms

By registering as a referee with FKF Meru League, you agree to abide by the following terms and conditions:

Registration Requirements
  1. Provide accurate and truthful information in your registration
  2. Maintain an active FKF license throughout your registration
  3. Keep your contact information updated in the system
  4. Notify the league of any changes to your availability or status
Professional Conduct
  1. Uphold the highest standards of integrity and impartiality
  2. Follow all FKF (Football Kenya Federation) rules and regulations
  3. Maintain professional conduct at all league activities
  4. Accept decisions made by the league management committee
Match Responsibilities
  1. Submit match reports accurately within 24 hours of match completion
  2. Attend mandatory training sessions and briefings
  3. Arrive punctually for all assigned matches
  4. Wear approved referee attire for all matches
Registration Approval

Your registration is subject to verification and approval by FKF Meru League administrators. You will be notified via email once your application has been reviewed. The approval process typically takes 1-2 business days.
